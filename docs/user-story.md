# User Stories

This document describes the primary user stories and their corresponding acceptance criteria for the **Tea and Hazelnut Harvest and Finance Management System**.

---

## US-01 – Create Harvest Seasons

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to create harvest seasons so that I can organize my records by season.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to create a new season.
- The user shall be able to enter a season name.
- The user shall be able to specify start and end dates.
- The system shall save the created season.
- The created season shall be displayed in the season list.

---

## US-02 – Record Tea and Hazelnut Harvests Separately

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to record tea and hazelnut harvests separately so that I can analyze each product individually.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to select tea or hazelnut as the product type.
- The system shall store records separately for each product.
- The user shall be able to filter records by product type.
- Reports shall display tea and hazelnut data separately.

---

## US-03 – Add Daily Harvest Records

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to add daily harvest records so that I can track my production amounts.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to enter the harvest date.
- The user shall be able to enter the harvest amount.
- The user shall be able to save the record.
- The system shall display the saved record.

---

## US-04 – Use Multiple Measurement Units

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to use different measurement units so that I can record harvests more accurately.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to choose kilograms, grams, or sacks.
- The selected unit shall be saved with the record.
- The system shall display the selected unit correctly.

---

## US-05 – Automatic Weight Calculation

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want the system to automatically calculate total weight so that I can avoid calculation mistakes.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The system shall automatically calculate total weight.
- The system shall perform unit conversions automatically.
- The calculated weight shall be displayed instantly.
- The system shall prevent calculation errors.

---

## US-06 – Record Transportation Expenses

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to record transportation expenses so that I can monitor my transportation costs.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to enter transportation costs.
- The user shall be able to enter expense descriptions.
- The system shall save transportation expenses.
- Transportation expenses shall appear in reports.

---

## US-07 – Record Labor Expenses

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to record labor expenses so that I can calculate my net profit accurately.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to enter labor expenses.
- The user shall be able to edit labor expenses.
- Labor expenses shall be included in total expenses.
- Labor expenses shall be shown in reports.

---

## US-08 – Record Fuel Expenses

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to record fuel expenses so that I can track all operational costs.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to enter fuel expenses.
- The system shall save fuel expense records.
- Fuel expenses shall be included in financial calculations.
- Fuel expenses shall appear in reports.

---

## US-09 – Enter Product Prices

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to enter product prices so that estimated revenue can be calculated.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to enter tea prices.
- The user shall be able to enter hazelnut prices.
- The user shall be able to update prices.
- The system shall save the entered prices.

---

## US-10 – Automatic Revenue Calculation

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want the system to automatically calculate my revenue so that I can monitor my earnings.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The system shall calculate total revenue automatically.
- Revenue calculations shall use product prices and harvest amounts.
- The calculated revenue shall be displayed to the user.
- Revenue values shall update automatically when data changes.

---

## US-11 – View Profit and Loss Analysis

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to view profit and loss analyses so that I can make better financial decisions.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The system shall calculate total expenses.
- The system shall calculate total revenue.
- The system shall display net profit or loss.
- The user shall be able to view financial summaries.

---

## US-12 – View Reports

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to view daily, monthly, and seasonal reports so that I can analyze my performance.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to view daily reports.
- The user shall be able to view monthly reports.
- The user shall be able to view seasonal reports.
- Reports shall include harvest and financial data.

---

## US-13 – Review Historical Records

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to review historical records so that I can compare previous seasons and performances.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The user shall be able to access previous records.
- The user shall be able to filter records by season.
- Historical records shall remain stored in the system.
- Historical records shall be displayed correctly.

---

## US-14 – View Dashboard

**User Story**

> As a Producer, I want to view a dashboard so that I can quickly access important statistics and summary information.

### Acceptance Criteria

- The dashboard shall display total harvest amounts.
- The dashboard shall display total expenses.
- The dashboard shall display total revenue.
- The dashboard shall display profit or loss information.
- The dashboard shall provide summary statistics.
